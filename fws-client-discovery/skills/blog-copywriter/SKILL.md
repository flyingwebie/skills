# Blog Copywriter — Production-Ready Blog Posts

## Purpose
Generate complete, SEO/GEO-optimized blog posts as individual markdown files. Each post targets a specific keyword from the content plan, is written for the correct buyer persona.

## When to Use
- After `/copywrite` has generated core website pages
- When executing Month 1-3 of the content plan
- When generating content for specific topic clusters

## CRITICAL: Prerequisites
Requires completed discovery data AND content plan. The blog posts are the "spoke" content that supports the pillar/service pages generated by `/copywrite`.

## Input
- Content plan (`08-Content-Plan.md`) — which blog posts to generate
- Discovery context — keywords, personas, competitors, FAQs
- Optional: specific blog posts to generate (by keyword or topic)

## Blog Post Types

| Type | Word Target | Purpose | Example |
|------|------------|---------|---------|
| How-To Guide | 1500-2500 | Informational, attract organic traffic | "How to Choose a Web Design Agency" |
| Listicle | 1200-2000 | Informational, shareable | "7 Signs Your Website Needs a Redesign" |
| Comparison | 1500-2500 | Commercial intent, evaluation stage | "WordPress vs Custom Website: Which Is Right?" |
| Industry Insight | 800-1500 | Thought leadership, EEAT | "Why [Industry] Businesses Are Investing in..." |
| Case Study Post | 1000-1800 | Social proof, decision stage | "How We Helped [Client] Increase Leads by 40%" |
| FAQ Deep-Dive | 1000-1500 | Question keywords, GEO optimization | "What Does [Service] Cost in [Year]?" |
| Local Focus | 800-1200 | Local SEO, community | "[Service] in [City]: A Complete Guide" |

## Blog-Specific Content Rules

### Opening Hook (first 100 words)
Must include one of:
- Surprising statistic
- Provocative question
- Bold statement
- Relatable pain point scenario
- Specific promise of what the reader will learn

### Body Structure
- **H2 every 300-400 words** for scannability
- **One key point per section** — don't ramble
- **Include data points** — specific numbers, percentages, timeframes
- **Internal links every 400 words** — to service pages, other blog posts, FAQ page
- **External references** (no links, just citations) for credibility

### Key Takeaways Section
- 3-5 bullet points summarizing the post
- Each takeaway is standalone and quotable
- GEO optimization: AI engines love concise takeaway sections

### FAQ Section (per blog post)
- 2-4 questions related to the blog topic
- Pull from FAQ research (Step 6)
- Direct answer pattern for Featured Snippet potential

### CTA
- Links to the parent pillar/service page
- Secondary link to contact page
- Aligned to the buyer journey stage of this post

## Internal Linking Strategy for Blog
- Every blog post links to its parent pillar page
- Every blog post links to 1-2 related blog posts
- Every blog post links to the most relevant service page
- Pillar pages should be updated to link to new blog posts

## Quality Checklist per Post
- [ ] Heading accent present (`:: keyword` line, 2-5 words, before H1)
- [ ] Primary keyword in heading accent, H1 (naturally), first 100 words, and 1-2 H2s
- [ ] Opening hook in first 100 words
- [ ] H2 every 300-400 words
- [ ] 3+ internal links (pillar page, service, related posts)
- [ ] At least 1 data point or statistic
- [ ] Key takeaways section
- [ ] FAQ section (2-4 questions)
- [ ] CTA links to pillar page + contact
- [ ] SEO metadata complete
- [ ] Word count meets type target
- [ ] Persona tone matches target audience
