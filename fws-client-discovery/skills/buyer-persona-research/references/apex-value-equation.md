# APEX Value Equation — Detailed Scoring Guide

## The Formula
```
Value = (Dream Outcome × Perceived Likelihood) / (Time Delay × Effort & Sacrifice)
```

## Scoring Each Component (1-10 Scale)

### Dream Outcome Score
How much does the buyer want this result?

| Score | Meaning | Signal |
|-------|---------|--------|
| 10 | Life-changing / Business-critical | "I'll go out of business without this" |
| 8-9 | Major impact on revenue or quality of life | "This would transform how we operate" |
| 6-7 | Significant improvement | "This would make things much better" |
| 4-5 | Nice to have | "We've been thinking about this" |
| 2-3 | Low priority | "Maybe someday" |
| 1 | Irrelevant | No interest |

**How to increase Dream Outcome**:
- Paint the vivid "after" picture
- Use specific numbers ("increase revenue by 40%")
- Connect to status/identity ("be known as the industry leader")
- Stack outcomes (primary + secondary + emotional)

### Perceived Likelihood Score
How much does the buyer believe you can deliver?

| Score | Meaning | Signal |
|-------|---------|--------|
| 10 | Absolute certainty | "Everyone I know uses them" |
| 8-9 | Very confident | "I've seen the case studies" |
| 6-7 | Cautiously optimistic | "Looks promising" |
| 4-5 | Skeptical but open | "I've been burned before" |
| 2-3 | Doubtful | "Sounds too good to be true" |
| 1 | Zero trust | "Another scam" |

**How to increase Perceived Likelihood**:
- Case studies with specific metrics
- Before/after comparisons
- Industry-specific testimonials
- Money-back guarantee or risk reversal
- Free trial or proof of concept
- Certifications and credentials

### Time Delay Score (Inverse — Lower is Better for Buyer)
How long until they see results?

| Score | Meaning | Buyer Tolerance |
|-------|---------|----------------|
| 1-2 | Immediate results | "I need this yesterday" |
| 3-4 | Days to weeks | "Quick turnaround expected" |
| 5-6 | 1-3 months | "Reasonable timeline" |
| 7-8 | 3-6 months | "Getting impatient" |
| 9-10 | 6+ months | "Way too long" |

**How to decrease Time Delay (perception)**:
- Quick wins in first week
- Milestone-based delivery
- Progress dashboards/updates
- "While we build, here's what you can do now"
- Break project into visible phases

### Effort & Sacrifice Score (Inverse — Lower is Better for Buyer)
How much work does the buyer have to do?

| Score | Meaning | Buyer Experience |
|-------|---------|-----------------|
| 1-2 | Done-for-you, turnkey | "I just approved and it was done" |
| 3-4 | Minimal involvement | "A few meetings and reviews" |
| 5-6 | Moderate collaboration | "Regular check-ins needed" |
| 7-8 | Significant time investment | "This is becoming a second job" |
| 9-10 | Major disruption | "We had to change everything" |

**How to decrease Effort & Sacrifice (perception)**:
- Done-for-you positioning
- Clear process with defined client touchpoints
- Templates and frameworks (don't start from scratch)
- Dedicated project manager
- "We handle everything, you just approve"

## Composite Value Score

Calculate: `(Dream × Likelihood) / (Delay × Effort)`

| Composite | Interpretation | Action |
|-----------|---------------|--------|
| > 5.0 | No-brainer offer | Lead with value, minimal selling needed |
| 3.0 - 5.0 | Strong value prop | Emphasize proof and quick wins |
| 1.5 - 3.0 | Competitive offer | Differentiate on speed or ease |
| 0.5 - 1.5 | Weak value prop | Major repositioning needed |
| < 0.5 | Don't bother | Wrong market or wrong offer |

## Persona-Specific Application

For each persona, map the Value Equation to specific **website elements**:

| Value Component | Website Element | Example |
|----------------|----------------|---------|
| Dream Outcome | Hero headline, benefits section | "Double your leads in 90 days" |
| Perceived Likelihood | Testimonials, case studies, badges | "Trusted by 200+ businesses" |
| Time Delay | Process section, timeline graphic | "3-phase delivery in 8 weeks" |
| Effort & Sacrifice | FAQ, process section, onboarding | "We handle everything" |

## Objection-to-Value Mapping

Every objection maps to a weak Value Equation component:

| Objection Type | Weak Component | Fix |
|---------------|---------------|-----|
| "Too expensive" | Dream Outcome too low | Increase perceived outcome value |
| "Will this actually work?" | Perceived Likelihood too low | Add more proof/guarantees |
| "How long will this take?" | Time Delay too high | Show quick wins, break into phases |
| "We don't have time for this" | Effort too high | Reposition as done-for-you |
| "We tried this before" | Likelihood too low | Address past failures directly |
| "Let me think about it" | Overall value not compelling | Stack more value, add urgency |
