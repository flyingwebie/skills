"Design Philosophy" — Numbered principles: (1) BEM for scoping, ACSS for values; (2) Section > Container > Content hierarchy; (3) Container queries, not media queries; (4) Vanilla JS with event delegation; (5) Per-component folders (index.html, style.css, script.js); (6) ACSS 4.x only, no 3.x patterns

1. "## Folder Structure" — `.etch-components/registry.json` at project root
